# Legal / Privacy Review Checklist

This checklist is for a legal/privacy reviewer. It does not approve launch by itself.

- [ ] Review `/policy` completeness.
- [ ] Confirm privacy policy title, scope and practical wording.
- [ ] Confirm consent wording for future live forms.
- [ ] Decide personal data operator questions.
- [ ] Decide whether operator identifiers may be published.
- [ ] Review personal data processing model for forms.
- [ ] Review storage and retention requirements.
- [ ] Review access control requirements.
- [ ] Review CRM processor role and contract requirements.
- [ ] Review analytics/cookie basis before enabling Metrica or other analytics.
- [ ] Review third-party services register.
- [ ] Review cross-border transfer questions.
- [ ] Review incident response process.
- [ ] Review publication disclaimers.
- [ ] Review public claims and advertising risk.
- [ ] Review IP/media/fonts/maps licenses.
- [ ] Confirm which legal identifiers are missing and remain HOLD.
- [ ] Decide what must be accepted before live forms.

## Required before live forms

- owner approval;
- legal/privacy approval;
- final consent text;
- privacy link near form;
- backend/CRM acceptance;
- retention/access-control decision;
- rendered evidence after implementation.
