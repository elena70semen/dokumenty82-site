## Purpose

This document defines the Stage 18D homepage product structure.

The homepage remains a local brand entry, router and safe lead-path entry. It must not become a service catalogue, price page, proof-claim page or live form page.

## Required Homepage Sections

| Section | Required role |
| --- | --- |
| Hero | Brand, local context, slogan and safe primary CTA. |
| Situation selector | Help the user choose the broad situation without listing every route. |
| Start path | Explain how work starts: describe, check inputs, choose route, continue safely. |
| Priority tasks | Show selected high-priority route families, not the whole registry as a dump. |
| Route groups | Provide structured navigation to approved hubs. |
| Client information | Explain what to bring or describe safely. |
| Local trust/NAP | Confirmed phone, address, local marker and route to contacts. |
| FAQ | Answer safe first-step/local/route-selection questions. |
| Final CTA | Close with `Разобрать ситуацию`, phone and contact fallback. |

## Homepage Description Direction

Preserve the direction:

`Центр подготовки документов в Симферополе. Разберём ситуацию и подготовим документы: регистрация, отчётность, ЕГРЮЛ, юридический адрес, банк и 115-ФЗ. Офис рядом с налоговой.`

Visible copy may be shorter or split across blocks, but must not contradict this direction.

## Homepage Must Remain

- Router;
- trust entry point;
- local brand entry;
- safe lead-path entry;
- semantic overview.

## Homepage Must Not Become

- giant service catalogue;
- hidden SEO page;
- price page;
- review/rating/case page;
- guarantee page;
- final legal proof page;
- live form page.

## CTA Boundary

The primary CTA is `Разобрать ситуацию`.

Secondary CTA paths may use:

- `Позвонить`;
- `Построить маршрут`;
- `Показать документы`.

No live submit, upload, CRM, analytics or messaging activation is approved.
